# [Internship Tracker](https://michaelgelooo.pythonanywhere.com/internship-tracker/ "Link to Internship Tracker Website")

## Project Overview

**Internship Tracker** is a centralized web-based platform designed to help students efficiently record, monitor, and manage their internship hours. The system simplifies daily time logging, automates hour calculations, and provides real-time progress tracking to ensure students stay aligned with their required internship hours.

---

## Project Functionalities

### 1. Daily Time Logging

* Allows students to:

  * Log **AM Time In and Time Out**.
  * Log **PM Time In and Time Out**.
  * Automatically calculate total hours rendered per day.
  * Use a quick logging feature for faster record entry.

### 2. Internship Hour Calculation

* Automatically:

  * Computes total rendered hours.
  * Tracks accumulated internship hours.
  * Calculates remaining required hours.
  * Generates internship statistics for performance monitoring.

### 3. Progress Monitoring Dashboard

* Displays:

  * Overall internship completion progress.
  * Total hours completed.
  * Remaining hours needed.
  * Visual indicators of progress status.

### 4. Monthly Time Record Table

* Provides:

  * A structured monthly daily time record.
  * A complete summary of logged entries.
  * Organized tracking for documentation and review purposes.
